Game Rules

01

Objective

Score more points than your opponent by potting balls in the correct sequence. Reds first, then colours, finishing with black.

02

Ball Values

Red (1), Yellow (2), Green (3), Brown (4), Blue (5), Pink (6), Black (7). Pot a red, then a colour, alternating until all reds are cleared.

03

Fouls

Potting the wrong ball, missing all balls, or potting the cue ball results in a foul. Minimum 4 points penalty to your opponent.

04

Break Building

Continue potting while legal shots are made. Your break ends when you fail to pot or commit a foul. Highest possible break is 147.

05

Final Colours

After all reds are potted, pot the colours in ascending order: yellow, green, brown, blue, pink, and finally black to end the frame.

06

Controls

Use mouse to aim and set power. Click to strike. Observe the cue ball carefully and plan your next shot strategically.
